A Little About Me

Hello! I'm Cooper Pflaum, a student at the University of Cincinnati majoring in Cybersecurity. My love for all things cybersecurity melds seamlessly with my enthusiasm for programming and developing various software applications. The challenge of ensuring digital security and the creativity involved in coding constantly inspire me to delve deeper into this fascinating field.

On top of my tech interests, I'm also an Eagle Scout, achieving this significant milestone on August 24, 2021. This experience instilled in me a love for camping and the great outdoors. I find solace and adventure in nature, and it's a perfect balance to my tech-focused pursuits. The blend of my passion for technology and the serenity of the outdoors is where I thrive and find my motivation to excel.
